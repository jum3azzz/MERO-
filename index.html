The difference

The Provenance Card

Every piece ships with its maker's story attached. A name, a cooperative, a region, a craft. Not authenticity in the abstract — a person.

Wine has terroir. Craft has hands. When you buy the work, you learn whose hands it passed through, and the card stays with the piece for as long as it exists.

Small batch, by design

Seasonal Drops

We do not hold inventory year-round. Collections arrive in small batches, timed to the Sudanese cultural calendar — Eid, the harvest, the cool season. When a drop sells out, it is gone. The scarcity is real because the production is.
